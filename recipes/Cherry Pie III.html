Cherry Pie III

Description

Delicious fruit dessert.


Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup shortening, chilled
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 cups pitted sour cherries
  • 11/4 cups white sugar
  • 10 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon almond extract
